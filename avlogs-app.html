Help & Tips

Everything you need to know about AVlogs

Frequently Asked
How do I get vlog ideas? ›

Go to the Home tab, type any topic (like "travel", "cooking", "fitness") and tap Generate. You'll instantly get 8 unique AI-powered vlog ideas!

Can I upload a photo to get ideas? ›

Yes! Go to the Upload tab, take a photo or upload an image, then tap "Get Vlog Ideas from This". The AI will analyze your image and suggest relevant vlog content.

Does audio/video upload work? ›

Yes! Upload audio or video files in the Upload tab. The AI will understand the context and suggest creative vlog ideas based on your content.

Why does the app ask for permissions? ›

Camera permission lets you take photos directly in the app. Microphone lets you use voice search. These are optional — you can skip and still use all features.

How do I use the voice search? ›

Tap the 🎙️ microphone icon in the top right. Speak your topic and it will auto-fill the search box and generate ideas for you!
